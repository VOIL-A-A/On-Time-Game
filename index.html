How To Play...

You need to get to Marcy On-Time! Run and grab all your things so can you make it to Mindful Morning. Pick up all the items you need for school to get a great score and make it On-time, but watch out for the snooze alarm that moves around! Did I also mention to not get distracted with tiktok?

  1. Use the arrow Keys to move around and the space bar to jump!
  2. Collect your materials for Marcy.
  3. Avoid TikTok so you dont get distracted!
  4. Don't touch the Alarms! But if you want to, jump on top of them for a jump boost!
  5. Find the Elivator to Go to the next level!
